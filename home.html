SECTION 2

ABCs of ERMOS
ERMOS

(engineering renewable microbial optimization solutions) is a collaborative e-learning

STEAM

(science, technology, engineering, art, math) and research platform remotely bringing the power of

MESS

(microbial electrochemical systems) to the masses with interconnected, experimental sets and an open-source platform.

Based on the most sustainable, state-of-the-art research projects, our interdisciplinary approach is built for students, scientists, educators, innovators, enthusiasts etc...

SECTION 7

meet the mini-mortals

We all know the saying, “so many microbes, so little time.” Now, there is enough time to discover and create ways that we can work with our invisible and/or overlooked friends to herald the change the world needs in order to live in harmony with the planet’s needs.

moss piglets

AKA water bears, one of the strongest organisms on earth and beyond with stubby legs and thrives all over the place.

salps

jet-propulsion carbon vacuum cleaners of the sea that communicate via bioelectric signals.

slime mold

a single-celled, multi-nucleus form of life that refuses to be classified. it is a transportation network expert.

moss

it’s pretty much everywhere and is great for plants and wood.

microalgae

a major contender for micro-scale renewable fuel.

lichen

like it or not, the application of lichens holds great promise from a biomedical and ecological perspective.
