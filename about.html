about

We’re a group of graduate students at CUNY working on sustainability in urban environments. We are experimenting with different applications and designs for MFCs and excited to share what we find.

We want to see these systems succeed and be adopted with ease for new developments and legacy structures. That’s why we’re building a predictive research platform and modular benchtop sandbox models. It includes all the resources, inspiration and information needed to get from research, development, publication and commercialization faster.
